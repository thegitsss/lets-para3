Payments & Escrow

How funds move safely between Attorneys and Paralegals using Stripe.

Stripe Escrow-style Hold Connect Payouts Disputes & Refunds
1Fund Escrow

When a job is posted with a flat fee, the Attorney pays through Stripe. Funds are charged and held for that job.

2Do the Work

Paralegal delivers per scope. Attorney reviews and can request revisions or open a dispute before release.

3Release & Payout

On approval, funds are released and automatically transferred to the Paralegal’s Stripe Connect account.

How it works under the hood

Funding

  • We create a Stripe Payment Intent or Checkout Session and attach a shared transfer_group.
  • Once paid, the job shows as Funded. The card is charged; funds are held pending completion.
Security: Cards are processed by Stripe. We never store card numbers.

Payouts

  • Paralegals complete Stripe Connect onboarding (KYC). Payouts go to their connected account.
  • On “Release,” we create a Transfer using the same transfer_group so Stripe links the flow end-to-end.
  • If onboarding isn’t finished yet, the payout waits until it is.
Timing: Bank clearing and Stripe payout schedules may add 1–3 business days.

Fees, taxes & refunds

  • Platform/service fees are shown in the posting flow (Attorney and/or Paralegal). Fees are deducted automatically.
  • Stripe fees apply per transaction under Stripe’s pricing.
  • Taxes: Users are responsible for applicable taxes. Stripe/Connect may collect tax information and issue forms where required.
  • Refunds: Before release, we can process refunds (e.g., job cancelled). After release, refunds are between the parties unless otherwise required.
  • Chargebacks: If a payment is reversed, we may debit/withhold amounts or offset future payouts to resolve balances.

Approval, revisions & disputes

  • Review window: Attorneys should review promptly and either approve or request revisions.
  • Open a dispute in the platform prior to release if the work doesn’t meet scope.
  • Resolution: We coordinate as a platform (not a law firm). We may recommend revisions or a refund prior to release.
Heads-up: After funds are released, reversing a payout is limited and may require mutual agreement.

FAQ

Do you store card numbers?
No. All card data is handled by Stripe. We store only Stripe IDs needed to link funding and payout.
What if a Paralegal hasn’t completed Stripe Connect?
They’ll be prompted to onboard. Funds remain unreleased until onboarding is complete.
Can I pay hourly?
Jobs are flat-fee by default in v1 to keep billing simple and predictable.
How long do payouts take?
After release, Stripe typically pays out within 1–3 business days depending on bank and country.
