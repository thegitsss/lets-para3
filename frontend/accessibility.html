Web Accessibility Statement

General

Let’s-ParaConnect cares about all users and is committed to making our platform accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to ensure that our site follows the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), and adheres to applicable laws such as the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

We use accessibility testing tools and user feedback to identify and fix issues that may interfere with access. Features include screen-reader support, keyboard navigation, and sufficient color contrast.

Disclaimer

While we strive to make our platform as accessible as possible, some third-party integrations may not be fully compliant. We continue to work towards a more inclusive experience for all users.

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our site, please contact us at admin@lets-paraconnect.com.
