Paralegal FAQ

Everything you need to know before joining our Founding Paralegal Collective.

1) What does Let’s-ParaConnect do for paralegals?

We help you find real paid work from verified U.S. attorneys — fully remote, fully flexible, and always secure.

2) Do I have to pay anything to join?

No. There are no subscriptions and no sign-up fees. We take a small platform fee from each completed job so the site stays free to use.

3) How do payments work?

Every job you accept is pre-paid by the attorney. The funds are held in Stripe escrow and are released to you as soon as the attorney marks the job complete.

4) Can I choose which jobs to apply for?

Yes. You are in complete control and only apply to the work that fits your interests.

5) Do I need to be certified?

Certification is helpful but not required. We approve paralegals based on experience and specialty fit.

6) How soon can I start working?

As soon as your profile is approved. Most approvals happen within 24–48 hours.

7) Do attorneys see my resume?

Yes. Résumés, skills, specialties, and software proficiency help attorneys evaluate your profile.

8) How are job rates handled?

Rates are set by the attorney and for now are flat-fee only.

9) How do I build repeat work?

When attorneys like your work, they can re-hire you directly on the platform to build recurring income.

10) What if there’s a dispute?

We offer a structured, neutral dispute process to protect both sides. Stripe holds funds until the issue is resolved.
