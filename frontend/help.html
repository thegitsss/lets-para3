Help & Support

Browse common questions about accounts, cases, messaging, and payouts.

1) Account & Login

Forgot your password? Reset it here. Still locked out? Make sure your browser isn’t auto-filling an old verification code if two-factor auth is on.

Need to update your email, profile, or security settings? Visit your Settings page or contact support.

2) Case Management

Attorneys can post a new case from the dashboard by selecting “Create New Case.” You’ll be prompted to enter scope, timelines, and invite paralegals.

Paralegals can browse invitations via “Browse Paralegals” or their dashboard. Active and archived work lives under Case Files.

3) Messaging & Collaboration

Each case has its own thread inside the Messages tab so conversations stay organized. Case cards display unread counts so you never miss an update.

Real-time collaboration and document annotations are on the roadmap—watch release notes for timing.

4) Payments & Escrow

How funds move safely between Attorneys and Paralegals using Stripe.

Funding the job

When an Attorney posts a job with a flat fee, Stripe charges the card or bank method at checkout. We store only the necessary Stripe IDs, never full card numbers.

Funds remain in a dedicated escrow bucket until the Attorney marks the work complete or a dispute is resolved.

Release & payout

Once the Attorney approves the deliverable, we create a Stripe Transfer to the Paralegal’s Stripe Connect account using the same transfer group.

Payouts usually settle within 2–3 business days, depending on the receiving bank.

Note: If the Paralegal hasn’t finished Stripe Connect onboarding, funds stay on hold until they complete KYC.

Fees, taxes, and refunds

  • Platform fees display in the case flow and are deducted automatically.
  • Stripe processing fees apply to each charge and payout.
  • Users are responsible for their own taxes. Stripe may collect additional forms when required.
  • Refunds can be processed before release. After release, refunds are handled between Attorney and Paralegal unless otherwise required.
  • If a payment is reversed (chargeback), we may withhold or offset future payouts.

Approval, revisions & disputes

  • Attorneys should review deliverables promptly.
  • Request revisions before releasing funds.
  • Open an in-platform dispute prior to release if the work doesn’t meet the agreed scope.
Reminder: After funds are released, reversing a payout is limited and often requires both parties’ agreement.

FAQ

Do you store card data? No. All sensitive payment information lives with Stripe; we retain only the Stripe IDs needed to link funding and payout.

What if a Paralegal hasn’t completed Connect? They’ll be prompted to finish onboarding, and funds remain on hold until they complete KYC.

Can I pay hourly? Version 1 focuses on flat-fee jobs to keep billing simple. Hourly support is on the roadmap.

How long do payouts take? After release, Stripe typically pays out within 1–3 business days depending on bank and country.
