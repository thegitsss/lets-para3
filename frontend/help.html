📝 Case Management

To post a new case, go to your dashboard and click “Create New Case.” You’ll be prompted to enter details, set deadlines, and invite a paralegal.

You can view active and completed cases from the “Cases” tab.

You can message your paralegal directly inside each case card. Unread messages will appear in your “Messages” tab.

Coming soon: real-time chat and document collaboration!

💳 Payments & Escrow

We hold all funds in secure escrow via Stripe. Payment is released once you confirm case completion.

Need billing support or have a different question? Contact us at admin@lets-paraconnect.com.
