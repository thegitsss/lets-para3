Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a job?

Attorneys can post jobs after logging into their dashboard by clicking “Create New Case” and following the steps provided.

How does payment work?

Payments are held in escrow through Stripe until the paralegal marks the job as complete and the attorney confirms. Then, funds are released.

Can I edit my profile?

Yes. Visit your profile section from your dashboard and click “Edit Profile” to update your details.
