Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Let’s-ParaConnect Inc. ("Let’s-ParaConnect," "LPC," "we," or "us") operates a platform that helps attorneys collaborate with vetted paralegals. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices available to you. By accessing the Platform, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly, such as account details, bar numbers, resumes, credentials, availability preferences, case notes, files, and messages. We also maintain technical and security data including device information, IP addresses, authentication tokens, and logs generated by the Platform. Payment interactions are powered by Stripe; we store Stripe identifiers (for example, checkout sessions, payment intents, or transfer references) but we never store complete card numbers.

3. How We Use Information

Information is used to operate the Platform, verify accounts, route collaboration requests, facilitate escrow and payouts, send transactional messages, enforce our Terms, and protect against fraud or abuse. We may use aggregated or de-identified data to analyze performance and improve features. We do not sell personal data.

4. Sharing & Service Providers

We share information with carefully selected processors who help us deliver the service— for example, MongoDB Atlas for databases, AWS for file storage, Stripe for payments, secure email partners for transactional notices, and Google reCAPTCHA for abuse prevention. These providers only access data to perform services on our behalf and must protect it consistent with this Policy. We may also disclose information when required by law, in response to legal process, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

5. Retention & Security

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the Platform, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Access to production systems is limited, encrypted, and monitored. While no online service is completely secure, we use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit, segmented data stores, access logs, and routine audits.

6. Your Choices & Rights

You may update account information within the application or by contacting support. If you wish to close an account or request deletion, email privacy@lets-paraconnect.com. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, portability, or objection) which we will honor consistent with applicable law.

7. International Transfers

Let’s-ParaConnect Inc. is based in the United States and processes data there. If you access the Platform from another region, you consent to the transfer of information to the United States or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. We rely on lawful mechanisms, such as Standard Contractual Clauses, when transferring data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland.

8. Children

The Platform is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children, and we will delete any data inadvertently collected if we become aware of it.

9. Updates & Contact

We may revise this Policy from time to time and will update the Effective Date when changes occur. Significant updates will be posted within the application or sent via email. Questions may be sent to privacy@lets-paraconnect.com or by mail to Let’s-ParaConnect Inc., 251 Little Falls Drive, Wilmington, DE 19808.
