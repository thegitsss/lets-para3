For Attorneys

A curated platform for project-based paralegal support.

Let’s-ParaConnect is a curated platform designed to help solo and small-firm attorneys engage experienced paralegals through structured, project-based work — without staffing agencies, full-time hiring commitments, or administrative friction.

The platform is intentionally selective. Both attorneys and paralegals are individually reviewed to ensure professionalism, experience, and alignment with real legal workflows.

1) Curated paralegal profiles

Paralegals on Let’s-ParaConnect are experienced professionals with prior law-firm and professional office experience. Each paralegal creates a detailed profile outlining their background, practice areas, and project experience. Admission is selective, and profiles are rigorously reviewed before approval.

2) Project-based work — not placement

Attorneys post defined legal projects, rather than open-ended roles or hourly employment arrangements. This structure allows attorneys to engage paralegal support on an as-needed basis — and to bill clients appropriately for paralegal time — without hiring full-time staff, paying salaries, or absorbing downtime associated with traditional employment.

Paralegals independently review postings and apply to work that aligns with their experience. Let’s-ParaConnect does not place, match, or assign paralegals. Attorneys retain full discretion over who they engage and how work is managed.

3) Clear scope before work begins

Each project includes a task overview so expectations are aligned from the outset. Final task details and workflow are confirmed after a paralegal is hired, allowing flexibility while maintaining clarity and control.

4) Escrow-backed payments

All projects are funded upfront through escrow, securely processed via Stripe. Funds are held until work is marked complete, providing transparency and protection for both attorneys and paralegals. This structure eliminates billing uncertainty and simplifies payment handling.

5) Independent, professional engagement

Paralegals work as independent professionals, not employees of the platform. Attorneys maintain control over scope, communication, and deliverables, while Let’s-ParaConnect provides the infrastructure for documentation, workflow clarity, and payments.

6) What Let’s-ParaConnect is — and isn’t

It is

  • A professional, escrow-backed platform for project-based legal work
  • Built specifically for solo and small-firm attorneys
  • Designed for experienced paralegals
  • Structured, transparent, and intentional

It is not

  • A staffing agency
  • A placement service
  • A job board
  • A high-volume marketplace
  • An open or public sign-up platform

Let’s-ParaConnect is a private, curated platform. Admission is limited and granted by approval only.

7) Is there a minimum case budget?

Yes. To maintain the standards of the platform, cases posted on Let’s-ParaConnect have a minimum compensation of $400. This reflects the experience level of our paralegals, who are vetted individually before admission, and supports focused, professional engagements rather than transactional or under-scoped work.

8) Are there platform fees?

Yes. Let’s-ParaConnect applies a 21% platform fee to completed cases. This fee supports the attorney and paralegal vetting process, secure escrow-based payment handling, and ongoing platform operations. All Stripe processing fees are covered by Let’s-ParaConnect, and no additional transaction charges are assessed.

9) Admission & availability

Attorney accounts are reviewed and approved manually. We are currently onboarding a limited group of founding attorneys ahead of escrow-backed project posting.
