3) Jobs, Escrow & Payments (Stripe)

The Platform uses Stripe to process and disburse funds:

  1. Funding Escrow. When an Attorney posts a flat-fee job, payment is authorized via Stripe Checkout. Funds are charged and held for that job (“Escrow”). We store Stripe IDs and set a shared transfer to link later payout.
  2. Release & Payout. On Attorney approval/marking complete, we create a Stripe Transfer to the Paralegal’s Stripe Connect account (less applicable platform fees) using the same transfer_group. If the Paralegal hasn’t completed Connect onboarding, payout is delayed until completion.
  3. Payment Model. Jobs are flat-fee by default unless expressly offered otherwise in the posting flow.
  4. Currency & Timing. Charges are processed in the currency shown at checkout (typically USD). Stripe processing and banking timelines apply.

By using the Platform, you authorize us and Stripe to process, hold, and disburse funds as described. All payments are subject to Stripe’s terms and risk controls.
