# Let's-ParaConnect Director Portal Guide

## Purpose

The Director Portal helps Directors of Attorney Relations track attorney outreach for Let's-ParaConnect.

Directors use Zoho Mail to send outreach emails. The Director Portal then tracks the outreach, attorney signups, matter activity, follow-ups, replies, and commission progress.

The portal is designed so directors do not need to manually create or organize records.

## Director Responsibilities

As a Director of Attorney Relations, your role is limited to:

1. Identify solo attorneys and small law firms that may benefit from Let's-ParaConnect.
2. Send the approved outreach email from your Let's-ParaConnect Zoho email account.
3. Keep the email template unchanged except for the attorney's name at the beginning.
4. Review the Director Portal to confirm outreach is being tracked.
5. Forward or flag attorney replies for Samantha if an attorney asks questions or needs help.

Do not modify the main outreach email body, footer, disclaimer, links, or signature unless Samantha instructs you to do so.

## Sending Outreach Emails

Outreach is sent from Zoho Mail, not directly from the Director Portal.

Use your assigned Let's-ParaConnect email account, such as:

`skyler@lets-paraconnect.com`

For each outreach email:

1. Open the approved outreach email template in Zoho.
2. Use "Edit as New" or the approved saved template.
3. Change only the greeting line, such as:

   `Hi Jordan,`

4. Leave the rest of the email unchanged.
5. Send the email to the attorney.

The portal will detect qualifying sent outreach emails automatically.

## Automatic Zoho Sync

The Director Portal automatically syncs with Zoho.

The system checks recent Zoho sent mail and inbox replies on a recurring schedule. When it finds a qualifying outreach email, it creates or updates the attorney's outreach record.

This means directors do not need to manually enter:

- Attorney name
- Attorney email address
- Outreach sent date
- Reply status
- Signup status
- Matter status
- Follow-up status
- Commission progress

Directors do need to select the attorney's state manually on each imported outreach row. Zoho email data does not reliably include the attorney's practice state.

## Manual Import

The Director Portal also includes a manual import option.

Use manual import only if:

- You just sent outreach and want to refresh the portal immediately.
- You believe the automatic sync has not picked up a recent email yet.
- Samantha asks you to re-import today's outreach.

Manual import is a fallback. The normal process is automatic syncing.

## What the Portal Tracks

The Director Portal tracks each attorney through the outreach lifecycle:

- Outreach sent
- Attorney registered
- Matter posted
- Follow-up auto sent
- Attorney replied
- Matter completed
- Commission earned

The portal updates based on Zoho activity and Let's-ParaConnect platform activity.

The State field is updated manually by the director after the outreach record appears.

## Attorney Signup Tracking

When an attorney signs up for Let's-ParaConnect using the same email address that received outreach, the portal automatically updates that attorney's record.

The director does not need to mark the attorney as registered.

## Matter Tracking

If a referred attorney posts a matter on Let's-ParaConnect, the portal automatically updates the attorney's record.

If a referred attorney completes a matter and LPC receives the attorney platform fee, the portal tracks commission eligibility.

## Automatic Follow-Up

If an attorney registers but does not post a matter within 8 days, LPC automatically sends the approved follow-up email.

The director does not need to manually send this follow-up.

After the automatic follow-up is sent, the attorney's record is categorized as:

`Follow-Up Auto Sent`

If the automatic follow-up fails, Samantha can see that from the Admin Director Oversight page.

## Replies From Attorneys

If an attorney replies to an outreach or follow-up email, the portal flags the reply for Samantha's attention.

Directors should not handle substantive attorney questions unless Samantha instructs them to do so.

If an attorney asks questions, requests help, or needs assistance, Samantha should review and respond.

## Commission Tracking

Directors earn commission only for attorneys they personally referred.

Commission is calculated from the attorney platform fee only.

The current commission structure is:

- LPC attorney platform fee: 22%
- Director commission: 50% of the attorney platform fee
- Director share: 11% of the matter amount

Directors do not earn commission from the paralegal platform fee.

Commission applies only to the first 50 completed commissionable matters assigned to that director.

Commission is earned only after:

1. The attorney registers for an attorney account.
2. The attorney is approved to use the platform.
3. The attorney completes a matter through Let's-ParaConnect.
4. LPC receives the attorney platform fee for that completed matter.

## Dashboard Metrics

The Director Portal includes dashboard metrics such as:

- Emails sent
- Registered attorneys
- Conversion rate
- Follow-ups sent
- Completed matters
- Outreach performance

These metrics update from imported Zoho outreach and platform activity.

## Date Ranges

The portal can show activity by date range, such as:

- Day
- Week
- Month

Use these views to understand recent outreach activity and performance.

## Admin Oversight

Samantha can view Director Oversight from the Admin Dashboard.

The admin oversight page shows:

- All directors
- Last Zoho sync status
- Replies needing Samantha's attention
- Failed follow-ups
- Duplicate assignments
- Commission audit details
- All director outreach records

The "Last Zoho sync" status shows whether Zoho syncing is healthy or needs attention.

## Best Practices

Use a consistent outreach process:

1. Focus on one state or market at a time.
2. Prioritize solo attorneys and small law firms.
3. Use the approved template exactly as provided.
4. Personalize only the attorney's name unless instructed otherwise.
5. Avoid sending duplicate outreach to the same attorney.
6. Allow the portal to track records automatically.

## What Directors Should Not Do

Directors should not:

- Change the outreach email body.
- Change the disclaimer, footer, signature, or social links.
- Promise approval to any attorney.
- Promise work to any attorney or paralegal.
- Negotiate platform terms.
- Respond substantively to attorney questions without Samantha's direction.
- Manually alter portal records unless Samantha instructs them to do so.

## Troubleshooting

If an outreach email does not appear in the portal:

1. Confirm the email was sent from the correct Zoho account.
2. Confirm the subject line matches the approved outreach subject.
3. Wait for the automatic sync to run.
4. Use manual import if needed.
5. Notify Samantha if the record still does not appear.

If an attorney reply is received:

1. Do not ignore it.
2. Make sure Samantha is aware.
3. Let Samantha handle questions, assistance requests, or platform concerns.

If the portal shows a sync issue, Samantha will review it from the Admin Director Oversight page.

## Summary

The director's main job is to identify qualified attorneys and send the approved outreach email through Zoho.

The Director Portal handles the tracking:

- Outreach records
- Attorney signups
- Matter activity
- Automatic follow-ups
- Replies for Samantha
- Commission progress

When used correctly, directors can focus on outreach while LPC handles the tracking and follow-up workflow automatically.
